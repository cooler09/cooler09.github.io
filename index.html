    About Us

    THE CROSSROADS GROUP, LLC

    THE CROSSROADS GROUP, LLC (TCG), is a full-service civil engineering and land surveying firm headquartered in Hamburg, Pennsylvania. The Crossroads Group, LLC has performed civil engineering and land surveying throughout the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, as well as project evaluations and designs in New York, Maryland, Delaware and West Virginia, New Jersey, Ohio, Virginia, and North Carolina. Our clientele ranges from single-family homeowners in need of grading and building permits, to nationally recognized telecommunication and energy companies in need of site surveying, design and permits. While our clients and their respective needs may vary, our high level of service does not. Every project is treated with the utmost level of engineering and project management excellence.

    TCG recognizes and addresses the various needs of our clients and the community. It is this flexibility; coupled with our attention to each client’s individual needs that distinguishes us from other engineering and land surveying firms. Our diverse client base includes:

    Residential Land Developers

    Residential land developers in need of fully engineered subdivision plans and associated permits.

    Commercial Land Developers

    Commercial land developers in need of fully engineered land development plans and associated permits.

    Non Profit Environmental Organizations

    Non-profit environmental organizations requiring fully engineered flood plain studies, reforestation surveys and wetland and/or groundwater recharge designs.

    Institutional Organizations

    Institutional organizations and places of worship in need of fully engineered land development plans and associated permits.

    Energy Companies

    Energy companies in need of site survey, site access design, erosion, and sedimentation control plans, and associated municipal, county and state permitting.

    Telecommunication Companies

    Telecommunication companies in need of site survey, lease exhibit plans, zoning plans, construction drawings and supporting PADEP land development approvals.

    Services

    The Crossroads Group, LLC has acquired Professional Engineering Licenses in New York, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, Ohio, and West Virginia. This geographic versatility provides our clientele with security in the knowledge that we can provide our high level of professional services throughout the Marcellus region.

    Site Surveys

    In support of various clients’ needs, TCG can provide topographic and boundary land surveying services as well as construction stakeout, ALTA/ACSM Survey Plans and title review.

    Construction Design Plans

    TCG provides full layout and design of proposed construction improvements and related approvals for well site locations. Our office will comprehensively approach the design with our many years of land development experience, minimizing delays and costs to the project.

    Municipal/County Approval

    Our experience expands well beyond the local area. TCG has performed engineering design services in over 30 counties within the Commonwealth as well as Maryland, West Virginia, and New York. Should additional plans be required, our experience to acquire any non-typical approvals will help our clientele to minimize time delays while still having construction ready plans.

    Chapter 102 Permitting

    In an ever changing regulatory environment, we have and continue to maintain steady interaction with regulatory agencies to ensure a quick and seamless approval process. Our experience in working throughout the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania has provided us with a very broad spectrum of understanding various regional nuances.

    Chapter 105 Permitting

    TCG has successfully prepared and acquired various General Permits for wetlands and or water crossings throughout Pennsylvania. It is our careful and determined coordination with the PADEP’s regional offices that has enabled us to provide quality engineering permits without impairing project timelines.

    County/PennDOT Permits

    TCG provides coordination of all construction related permits required by the local municipality, county and state. Our experience ranges from minimum use driveways permits to high volume driveways requiring Highway Occupancy Permits by the Department of Transportation. Our office is extremely knowledgeable in the coordination and implementation of all access permits necessary to support site develop

    A/E Telecom

    TCG expanded its services in 2008 to include A/E Telecom Site design for small cells and towers alike. With the utilization of our other divisions, we also offer a multitude of services including site audits, engineering evaluations, site survey plans, FAA Certifications, zoning drawings, carrier-specific designs, NEC clearance analysis, utility coordination, and much more. We have designed over 400 small cells in Pennsylvania, as well as many new (rawland) sites.

    Team

    While our offices are spread throughout Pennsylvania, our small, family owned roots remain as we support our clients throughout the industries. Read more about what makes each office unique below.

    Headquarters

    TCG’s Hamburg office is the main headquarters for The Crossroads Group. As the largest of the three offices, they offer a full array of all our services. What started in Quakertown, PA, has strategically relocated for easy accessibility to land development and telecom sites throughout the Pennsylvania.
    The culture of this office, and TCG as a whole, is that of many family owned businesses. We work diligently with our clients to achieve their goals in a timely and cost-effective manner. We maintain this ethic for our teams as to encourage and improve upon individual skills and improve quality of life. The Hamburg office enjoys friendly competitions and social events. They also support the local community through sponsorships and volunteer time for events such as the Hamburg-er Festival, Art of the Brew Festival, The King Frost Parade, and collaborating with Hamburg Area High School’s mentorship program.

    Paxinos Branch

    TCG’s Paxinos office was first established in Elysburg, PA, after the acquisition of Richard C. Parsons Land Surveying in November 2011. This team has since grown and moved just down the road to the Bebenek ‘Crossroads’ Plaza in Paxinos, a truly fitting location! The Paxinos team offers full engineering services in addition to the mainstay surveying that got this office started. On the occasional Friday, you can find this group taking a break from the typical day to day tasks by enjoying egg sandwiches for breakfast and watching movie trailers. As can be said for all TCG offices, we have an open-door policy. However, in the Paxinos community, more so than others, friends, neighbors, and clients actively use it to stop by and have a good, old-fashioned discussion and we love it!

    Pittsburgh Branch

    TCG’s Pittsburgh office was established in November 2018. Although this office started out as a small remote group of 3, after adding a few new team members, they are now officially established in Bridgeville, PA. While the Pittsburgh office specializes in wireless telecommunications, their abilities extend to fiber engineering and data collection/field audits in the tri-state area of Western PA, Central/East Ohio, and West Virginia. Additionally, the team works to support both the survey and land development services in Hamburg and Paxinos. TCG Pittsburgh believes that the best way to break up work tension is a visit from our furry friends or a potluck style lunch. We whole heartedly believe and support the notion that happy employees willingly go the extra mile to make happy clients.

    Nicholas Duchan

    P.L.S., Director of Land Surveying

    Nick has been with The Crossroads Group since October 2014 and has more than 24 years of experience in all phases of surveying required to support engineers designing local roads, residential subdivision, commercial, & land development projects, as well as boundary resolutions & construction stakeout.
