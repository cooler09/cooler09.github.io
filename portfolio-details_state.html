State Street Apartments

The State Street Apartment complex is a 50-unit apartment development located on State Street in the Borough of Hamburg, Pennsylvania. The design incorporated and repurposed the old, abandoned Hamburg Elementary School in harmony with proposed new construction. TCG was contracted to fully design this land development. Our services included site layout, dwelling unit layout, traffic and parking layout and design, site grading, stormwater and post-construction stormwater management, erosion and sedimentation control design, and landscaping/lighting design. TCG was responsible for obtaining the required approvals/ permits from Penn DOT (H.O.P. for site access and utility connections), DEP (Planning Modules and NPDES), Berks County Conservation District (E&S approval), and Hamburg Borough Council. The site is actively under construction and expects be operating within the following year.
