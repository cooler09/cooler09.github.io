Team

While our offices are spread throughout Pennsylvania, our small, family owned roots remain as we support our clients throughout the industries. Read more about what makes each office unique below.

Headquarters

TCG’s Hamburg office is the main headquarters for The Crossroads Group. As the largest of the three offices, they offer a full array of all our services. What started in Quakertown, PA, has strategically relocated for easy accessibility to land development and telecom sites throughout Pennsylvania.
The culture of this office, and TCG as a whole, is that of many family owned businesses. We work diligently with our clients to achieve their goals in a timely and cost-effective manner. We maintain this ethic for our teams as to encourage and improve upon individual skills and improve quality of life. The Hamburg office enjoys friendly competitions and social events. They also support the local community through sponsorships and volunteer time for events such as the Hamburg-er Festival, Art of the Brew Festival, The King Frost Parade, and collaborating with Hamburg Area High School’s mentorship program.

Elysburg Branch

TCG’s Elysburg office was first established in Elysburg, PA, after the acquisition of Richard C. Parsons Land Surveying in November 2011. This team has since grown and moved just down the road to the Bebenek ‘Crossroads’ Plaza in Elysburg, a truly fitting location! The Elysburg team offers full engineering services in addition to the mainstay surveying that got this office started. On the occasional Friday, you can find this group taking a break from the typical day to day tasks by enjoying egg sandwiches for breakfast and watching movie trailers. As can be said for all TCG offices, we have an open-door policy. However, in the Elysburg community, more so than others, friends, neighbors, and clients actively use it to stop by and have a good, old-fashioned discussion and we love it!

Pittsburgh Branch

TCG’s Pittsburgh office was established in November 2018. Although this office started out as a small remote group of 3, after adding a few new team members, they are now officially established in Bridgeville, PA. While the Pittsburgh office specializes in wireless telecommunications, their abilities extend to fiber engineering and data collection/field audits in the tri-state area of Western PA, Central/East Ohio, and West Virginia. Additionally, the team works to support both the survey and land development services in Hamburg and Elysburg. TCG Pittsburgh believes that the best way to break up work tension is a visit from our furry friends or a potluck style lunch. We whole heartedly believe and support the notion that happy employees willingly go the extra mile to make happy clients.

Leadership

Jeremiah D. Hoagland

President

Jeremy was raised on a working farm in central PA after crashing on earth in what appeared to be an inter-galactic life sustaining escape vessel. After achieving maximum saturation level from the solar system’s yellow sun, he realized his real potential, instantly leading his high school football team to a state championship & dominating a multitude of gladiatorial championships. Flying faster than a speeding bullet to Wilkes University, where he not only continued displaying his athletic prowess, but stunned his professors with his intellect & dazzled the co-eds. Upon graduation he entered the Civil Engineering field, where he strives to bring those guilty of bureaucratic red tape to justice through super-human manipulation of stormwater runoff.

Joshua D. Hoagland

Vice President

Brother of one, second to none, our Vice President, Joshua Hoagland excels at fighting black bears (albeit under 300 lbs). Josh joined the company in 2008 after graduating Johns Hopkins University with a degree in Civil Engineering and worked his way up to obtaining his Professional Engineer license, managing projects, and eventually becoming VP. When he is not ridding the wilderness of dangerous bears, he likes to design subdivisions and commercial land developments. He has worked all throughout bear territory including Ohio, West Virginia, Maryland, and New York to name a few of our service areas outside of Pennsylvania. (He has yet to win a fight with a Grizzly Bear. It’s a work in progress.)

Ron Bobiak

VP, Director of A&E Services

With 14 years Telecommunications experience & a few gray hairs to accompany them, Ron serves as a partner with TCG. In 2017, he was given a gift that few people can truly understand. After a fateful day of yard work, he was unknowingly bitten by a [radioactive] Brown Recluse spider. Within days of the bite, it was apparent that surgery was necessary. As he healed, Ron discovered that he now had the power to live in the present & laugh at the silly claims by his business partners about bears & escape vessels. He also found a renewed strength of body & mind to fight injustices by ne’er do wells & scallywags alike. He now seeks to mentor young professionals to see their potential to succeed – in life & business. When not combating evil, Ron enjoys time at home with his wife & two rescue dogs, playing guitar, reading, or barbequing on the smoker.

Becky Grant

Director, Real Estate Services

Becky joined The Crossroads Group in July 2021 after moving to Pittsburgh from the DC Metro area. She brings with her telecom-centric real estate and construction expertise after serving the Washington/Baltimore market for 13 years – 10 of those spent overseeing AT&T site development. When she looks around the table, she’s happy to know that’s exactly what she’s found – surrounded by the good guys. When she’s not over-organizing, jotting down copious (read: ridiculous) amounts of meeting minutes, or cheering on her colleagues while they wrestle bears/avoid spiders/survive inter-galactic crashes – she can be found enjoying the great outdoors, volunteering at church, or hanging with her rescue pup George.
